G a u g e
The market isn't crashing—It's speaking. Gauge is a new publishing platform dedicated to examining the art market through the eyes of those who sustain it. Through anonymous contributions, interviews, and essays, Gauge creates a forum where collectors can interrogate the pressure systems of the art world that aim at influencing their choices. Gauge exists to help people find their way back to art by exposing, discussing, and look back at the consequences that have quietly convinced them to stay away.
