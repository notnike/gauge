Gauge
1

Gauge is a new publishing platform dedicated to examining the mechanisms that shape the art market—through the eyes of those who sustain it. Through anonymous contributions, critical essays, and structured provocations, Gauge creates a forum where collectors can interrogate the pressure systems of the art world that influence their choices. Gauge exists to help people find their way back to art—by exposing the forces that have quietly convinced them to stay away.
